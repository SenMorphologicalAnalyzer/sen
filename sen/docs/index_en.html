Sen (Morphological Analyzer)

What is Sen?

Sen is a the open source Japanese morphological analyser. Sen derives from ChaSenTNG which is written by C++. Takashi Okamoto rewrote the ChaSenTNG with Java. It was purposed to use Japanese by jakarta lucene. But it may be useful fo National Language Processing.

Origin of Software Name

There are two origin.

In the first, 'ChaSen' is the most famous Japanese morphological analzer. I was not professional for Natural Language Processing, so I thought my morphological analyzer can't win ChaSen. So, I just removed 'Cha' from 'ChaSen'.

Second, currently Sen supports only Japanese but if someone prepares the dictionary for other language, Sen would be able to treat additional language. The author hope sen will grow international morphological analyzer. Then, he thinks it's excelent that a lot of people know Japanese good culture through his software. So he named Sen which is name of heroine of the most famous movie 'Sen to Chihiro no kamikakushi' (The title in English is 'The Spirited Away') in Japan. Even neither Star Wars nor E.T was able to beat 'Sen to Chihiro no Kamikakushi' in Japan. Chihiro no Kamikakushi is not evaluated only Japanese but also european. In the fact, Sen took 'golden bear prize' at Berlin movie festival.

If you want to know more detail about Sen to Chihiro no Kamikakushi, please see http://www.nausicaa.net.
